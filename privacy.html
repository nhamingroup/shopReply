1. Gioi thieu

ShopReply la mot Chrome Extension giup cac chu shop online tu dong tra loi tin nhan khach hang tren Facebook Messenger va Zalo. Extension su dung tri tue nhan tao (AI) chay hoan toan tren may tinh cua ban de goi y cau tra loi dua tren co so du lieu hoi-dap ma ban tu tao.

Chung toi cam ket bao ve quyen rieng tu cua ban. Chinh sach nay giai thich cach ShopReply xu ly du lieu cua ban.

Nguyen tac cot loi: Moi du lieu cua ban deu duoc xu ly va luu tru TAI MAY TINH CUA BAN. Khong co du lieu nao duoc gui den may chu ben ngoai.

2. Du lieu chung toi truy cap

ShopReply truy cap cac du lieu sau chi tren trinh duyet cua ban:

  • Tin nhan chat: Doc noi dung tin nhan tren Facebook Messenger va Zalo de hieu cau hoi cua khach hang.
  • Co so du lieu hoi-dap: Cac cap cau hoi-tra loi ma ban tu tao va nhap vao extension.
  • License key: Ma kich hoat ban quyen duoc luu tai may.

Quan trong: ShopReply KHONG thu thap bat ky du lieu phan tich (analytics), do luong su dung (telemetry), hay bat ky du lieu theo doi nao.

3. Luu tru du lieu — 100% tai may cua ban

Toan bo du lieu duoc xu ly va luu tru tai may tinh cua ban:

  • Co so du lieu Q&A: Luu trong file SQLite tai may cua ban.
  • Xu ly tin nhan: Thuc hien boi backend server chay tai localhost:3000 tren may cua ban.
  • AI/LLM: Neu ban su dung Ollama, mo hinh AI cung chay hoan toan tai may (local).
  • License key: Luu tru trong bo nho cuc bo cua extension (Chrome storage).

Khong co du lieu nao duoc truyen den bat ky may chu ben ngoai nao.

4. Chia se du lieu

Chung toi KHONG chia se, ban, cho thue, hay chuyen giao bat ky du lieu ca nhan nao cua ban cho bat ky ben thu ba nao.

ShopReply khong su dung cookie hoac bat ky cong nghe theo doi nao.

5. Dich vu ben thu ba

ShopReply chi tuong tac voi hai dich vu ben ngoai:

a) Xac thuc ban quyen (License Validation)

Khi kich hoat license, extension gui chi license key va device ID den mot Cloudflare Worker de xac minh. Khong co du lieu ca nhan hay du lieu chat nao duoc gui.

b) Thanh toan (Payment Processing)

Viec thanh toan duoc xu ly boi:

  • LemonSqueezy — cho thanh toan quoc te (the tin dung, PayPal, v.v.)
  • SePay / VietQR — cho chuyen khoan ngan hang tai Viet Nam

Extension khong bao gio tiep can thong tin thanh toan cua ban. Moi giao dich duoc xu ly truc tiep boi cac nen tang thanh toan noi tren theo chinh sach bao mat rieng cua ho.

ShopReply khong huong den tre em duoi 13 tuoi. Chung toi khong co chu dich thu thap du lieu ca nhan tu tre em. Neu ban la phu huynh va phat hien con ban dang su dung extension nay, vui long lien he voi chung toi.

English Version

1. Introduction

ShopReply is a Chrome Extension that helps online shop owners automatically reply to customer messages on Facebook Messenger and Zalo. The extension uses artificial intelligence (AI) running entirely on your local computer to suggest replies based on a Q&A database you create yourself.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how ShopReply handles your data.

Core principle: All your data is processed and stored ON YOUR COMPUTER. No data is ever sent to any external server.

2. Data We Access

ShopReply accesses the following data only within your browser:

  • Chat messages: Reads message content on Facebook Messenger and Zalo to understand customer questions.
  • Q&A database: Question-answer pairs that you create and import into the extension.
  • License key: Your activation license key, stored locally.

Important: ShopReply does NOT collect any analytics, telemetry, or tracking data whatsoever.

3. Data Storage — 100% Local

All data is processed and stored on your own computer:

  • Q&A database: Stored in a local SQLite file on your machine.
  • Message processing: Handled by a local backend server running at localhost:3000 on your computer.
  • AI/LLM: If you use Ollama, the AI model also runs entirely on your machine (local).
  • License key: Stored in the extension's local storage (Chrome storage).

No data is ever transmitted to any external server.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or transfer any of your personal data to any third party.

ShopReply does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

5. Third-party Services

ShopReply only interacts with two external services:

a) License Validation

When activating your license, the extension sends only the license key and device ID to a Cloudflare Worker for verification. No personal data or chat data is sent.

b) Payment Processing

Payments are handled by:

  • LemonSqueezy — for international payments (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
  • SePay / VietQR — for Vietnamese bank transfers

The extension never has access to your payment information. All transactions are processed directly by the payment platforms above under their own privacy policies.

6. Permissions Explained

Below are the permissions ShopReply requires and why:

Permission Purpose
storage Save extension settings and license key locally on your device.
tabs Detect when you are on a Facebook Messenger or Zalo page to activate auto-reply features.
notifications Send notifications when new messages need replies or when there are status updates.
host_permissions Access Facebook Messenger and Zalo page content to read chat messages and insert suggested replies.

7. Children's Privacy

ShopReply is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not intentionally collect personal data from children. If you are a parent and discover that your child is using this extension, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the extension after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
